Why Meshtastic

Off-grid communication that just works

No infrastructure required. Your messages hop from node to node until they reach their destination.

No Cell Service Needed

Works in remote areas, during outages, or anywhere traditional networks fail. Your messages get through.

End-to-End Encrypted

AES-256 encryption ensures your messages stay private. Even relay nodes can't read your content.

Long Battery Life

Low-power LoRa radios run for days on a single charge. Solar-powered nodes can run indefinitely.

Multi-Mile Range

Individual nodes can reach several miles. With mesh routing, your messages can travel across the entire valley.

Community Powered

Get Started

Join the mesh in four steps

Getting on the network is straightforward. Here's how to get your first node online.

1

Get Hardware

Pick up a Meshtastic-compatible device. We recommend the Heltec V3 or RAK WisBlock for beginners.

2

Flash Firmware

Install the latest Meshtastic firmware using the web flasher. No coding required.

3

Configure

Set your region to US 915MHz and configure your node name. Join the SV Mesh channel.

4

Deploy

Place your node in a good location — higher is better! Connect with the community.
